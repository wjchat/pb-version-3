Humano the Tribe

Photography + Videography

Humano the Tribe attempts capture the essence of the human spirit. They create strong communities of diverse individuals seeking to use tools such as arts, culture, and music to spark human progression.

Prettyboy Creative was tasked with documenting this community at Humano's annual trip to Burning Man Festival in addition to showcasing the custom infrastructure built for their camp.

An album of 200 photos and a 4 minute recap video of the trip were delivered to recreate the visceral experience of participating in the burn of the Black Rock Desert.
